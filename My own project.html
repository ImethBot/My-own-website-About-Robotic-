What is technology?

Technology is the use of scientific knowledge to create tools, techniques, and processes that help people solve problems, improve their lives, and achieve goals. It can include simple tools like hammers and wheels, or complex systems like computers and the internet.

Electronic technology

Electronic technology is the design, production, and maintenance of electronic devices, systems, software, and control circuits. It's used in many areas of daily life and industrial production, including:

  • Computer aided circuit design and production
  • Radio and TV technologies
  • Civil and military communication systems
  • Satellite communication systems
  • Factories that manufacture industrial electronic devices

Mechanical technology

Mechanical technology is the application of engineering principles and scientific concepts to the design, operation, and testing of machines and equipment. It involves practical skills and the creation of useful products and production machinery.

Medical technology

Medical technology is the use of scientific knowledge and tools to diagnose, treat, and improve a person's health. It can include a variety of devices and software

communications technology

Communication technology, also known as Information and Communication Technology (ICT), is a broad term for the tools and systems that allow people and organizations to interact and exchange information:

  • Devices: Computers, landlines, and mobile phones
  • Applications: Video chat, messenger, social media, and email
  • Other technologies: The internet, wireless networks, software, middleware, and live and recorded broadcasting technologies

industrial and manufacturing technologies

Industrial and manufacturing technologies are a set of technologies and methodologies that are used to improve the efficiency, quality, and productivity of industrial processes
